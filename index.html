RS Study: Master’s thesis

“The influence of preference elicitation to micro-moment recommendations:
An interactive MMRS”

Introduction:

The theme is the design of the recommendation system,
which belongs to the field of human-computer interaction.

Combined with the concept of Micro-moments proposed by Google,
a three-phase user study is designed to explore how different types of user preferences affect the RS in terms of recommendation system performance and user perception in the context of high intention and urgency.

Considering the ease of use, Line chatbot served as a micro-moment recommendation system to interact with users of four groups.
